What's included

Everything you need to start earning in under five minutes.

Menu bar app

Lives in your menu bar, never in the Dock. Click to see live CPU, GPU, and ANE utilization, active jobs, and earnings.

Smart routines

Set it to run only on AC power, only when you're idle, or only between certain hours. Pauses automatically when conditions aren't met.

Job history

Every completed and failed job is logged locally with duration, engine used, and timestamps so you can see exactly what ran.

Auto-connect

Sign in once and the app reconnects silently every time you restart your Mac — no manual setup required.

Launch at login

One toggle in Settings installs a login item so the app starts with macOS and your Mac is always ready to accept work.

Signed & sandboxed

Install in 5 steps

Download. Open. Sign in. Set limits. Start earning.

1

Download the DMG

Click "Download for Mac" above. The .dmg is signed and notarized — Gatekeeper will clear it automatically.

2

Open & install

Open the DMG and drag Common Compute.app into your Applications folder.

3

Launch & sign in

Open the app from Applications. It appears in your menu bar. Enter your email and password to sign in.

4

Set your limits

In Settings, choose when to accept work — AC-only, idle-only, time windows, and which task priorities.

5

Start earning

The menu bar icon turns green. Verified batch jobs arrive automatically when your Mac meets the conditions you set.

System requirements

Built for Apple Silicon. Runs on Intel too.

macOS 14 Sonoma or later

Requires macOS 14+ for SwiftUI menu bar APIs and SMAppService login-item management. Free upgrade from Apple.

Apple Silicon (recommended)

M1 through M4 chips unlock ANE and GPU runners for the highest-value tasks. Intel Macs run CPU-class workloads.

8 GB RAM minimum

16 GB or more enables LLM inference tasks. 24 GB unlocks image generation. More RAM = more work types available.

Common Compute is signed with Developer ID and notarized by Apple for secure installation outside the Mac App Store. Gatekeeper verifies the build on first launch so you know the app has not been tampered with. The app is free to install and earns on your behalf in the background.
