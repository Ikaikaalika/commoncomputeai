# Common Commute Brand Direction

## Core positioning

Common Commute turns idle Macs into a verified compute network for affordable AI workloads.

### Short version

Idle Macs. Useful compute. Fair payouts.

### Alternative positioning lines

- Run AI workloads for less. Earn from idle Mac time.
- Affordable AI compute powered by Apple Silicon.
- A trusted network for batch AI jobs and passive Mac earnings.
- Verified distributed compute for embeddings, transcription, OCR, and more.

## Brand pillars

### Trust

- Transparent usage
- User control
- Verified jobs
- No hidden activity

### Efficiency

- Cheaper AI workloads
- Better utilization of existing hardware
- Lower waste

### Fairness

- Fair to compute providers
- Fair to compute buyers

### Practicality

- Lower cost
- Easy setup
- Safe execution
- Predictable outcomes

## Tone guidelines

Use:
- Plain English
- Short declarative sentences
- Calm confidence
- Concrete verbs

Avoid:
- Hype
- Buzzword overload
- Revolutionary or disruptive language
- Decentralized as a lead term unless speaking to a technical audience

## Homepage copy skeleton

### Hero

Affordable AI compute, powered by idle Macs

Run verified batch AI workloads for less or earn from the Apple Silicon you already own.

### Supporting line

Built for embeddings, transcription, OCR, and large batch jobs that do not need expensive always-on cloud infrastructure.

### Trust bar

- Signed and notarized macOS app
- Provider controls for power and schedule
- Verified task completion
- Transparent earnings and usage

### Provider section

Earn from your idle Mac.

Common Commute runs only within the limits you choose. Set schedules, pause anytime, and keep control of your machine.

### Customer section

Lower-cost batch AI workloads.

Submit jobs through the dashboard or API and get verified results without paying premium cloud prices for every workload.

### Why Common Commute

Cheaper than premium cloud for the right workloads.

Common Commute is designed for batch jobs, not ultra-low-latency interactive inference.

### How it works

1. Providers install the Mac app
2. Customers submit jobs
3. Work is matched, executed, and verified
4. Results are returned and providers get paid

### Final CTA

Use spare compute better.

Whether you want to lower AI processing costs or earn from idle hardware, Common Commute gives both sides a fairer option.

## Download page copy

### Recommended headline

Download Common Commute for Mac

### Recommended trust copy

The Common Commute Mac app is signed with Developer ID and notarized by Apple for direct distribution outside the Mac App Store. That helps Gatekeeper verify the app when users download and open it.

### Recommended helper text

After download:
1. Open the app
2. Sign in or create an account
3. Choose your resource limits
4. Start earning when your Mac is idle

## Mac app release path

- Join the Apple Developer Program
- Sign the app with a Developer ID certificate
- Notarize it with Apple
- Staple the notarization ticket
- Package it for download
- Host it on your site or CDN
